INSTRUCTIONS





1. ASTAROT involves the Dragon flying towards his lair while dodging the obstacles that comes throughout his journey.

2. Use the spacekey or the mouse to make the Dragon fly and dodge the obstacles.

3. As you move further, the Score increases , so does the speed of the game .

4. Once the Dragon collides with the obstacle or falls to the ground , the game stops.

5. Press the 'Play Again?' button to restart the game.

6. Once the game restarts , the score automatically sets back to zero.

Enjoy Playing!
